About

We are a multidisciplinary competitive robotics team at UBC, Vancouver.

We compete annually in RoboMaster, one of the world's most technically and strategically intensive robotics competitions hosted by DJI in Shenzhen, China.

Through competitive robotics, our members develop expertise and enthusiasm in robotics, design, embedded systems, and computer vision.

FAQ

How can I join UBC RoboMaster?


Our winter recruitment cycle has now closed. Check back in early September for fall recruitment information. As well, follow us on Facebook for updates and outreach events.

What time commitment is expected?

The team holds weekly meetings on Sunday to present updates with additional subteam meetings throughout the week. We recommend a minimum time commitment of 10 hours a week to become sufficeintly involved. As competition deadlines approach, the workload will naturally increase.

Where can I find more about the competition?

The official RoboMaster website contains many useful information on how the competition works. You can also checkout their official Twitch channel for exciting match footage!

Who are you looking for?


This team is open to all UBC students. We are looking for enthusiasm in robotics, an eagerness to learning and a problem solving mindset. Our four main streams include mechanical design, embedded systems, vision algorithms, and outreach projects.
