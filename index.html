About

We are a multidisciplinary competitive robotics team at UBC, Vancouver.

Due to restrictions imposed by the pandemic, we have switched focus to the software-centered RoboMaster AI challenge this year.

We highly value the development of our members, and strive to fulfill their curiosity and desire to learn in the context of a robotics competition.

FAQ

Contact us if you have any further questions

Where can I find more about the competition?

The official RoboMaster AI challenge webpage contains a brief overview and detailed competition rules. You can also find past competition footage and team submissions on YouTube.

Who are you looking for?


Due to our shifted focus, we are only recruiting for software positions this year. While we highly value a curious and eager mindset, a moderate level of programming experience is recommended for the problems we are tackling.

What time commitment is expected?

Aside from short update meetings on Sundays which members are expected to attend, we don't enforce a time commitment. Our members know that the time they commit directly affects their involvement and learning.

How can I join UBC RoboMaster?


Our fall recruitment has started and will remain open until September 20th. Soon after you submit the application, we will reach out to schedule a meeting with you.
